Hello, I'm Aria

I quit a corporate desk job in 2019 to live out of a single backpack. Since then, I've chased monsoons across Southeast Asia, slept in monasteries in the Himalayas, and eaten my way through 40 countries — all while writing it down so you don't miss a single detour.

This journal is my honest, unfiltered account of the world as I find it — the breathtaking and the broken, the expensive and the free.
